# Instant Payments Product Specification

## Overview

Instant Payments allows retail and small business customers to send domestic
payments that settle within seconds.

## Features

- Real-time beneficiary validation.
- Confirmation of payee before submission.
- Immediate ledger reservation and posting.
- Push notification after settlement confirmation.
- Daily limits based on customer segment and risk score.

## Success Metrics

- 99.5 percent of payments confirmed within 15 seconds.
- Less than 0.5 percent customer support contact rate.
- No increase in confirmed fraud losses above approved tolerance.
