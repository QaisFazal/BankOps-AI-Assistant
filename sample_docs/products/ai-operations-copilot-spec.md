# AI Operations Copilot Product Specification

## Overview

The AI Operations Copilot helps bank operations teams summarize incidents,
retrieve runbooks, draft customer support updates, and identify likely next
actions.

## Initial Scope

- Search approved incident reports, runbooks, policies, and architecture notes.
- Summarize operational status from user-provided text.
- Suggest relevant runbook sections with citations.
- Show agent activity, including retrieval and validation steps.

## Out of Scope

The copilot does not execute payment overrides, change feature flags, approve
customer refunds, or update production systems without a separate human-approved
workflow.
