# Payment Data Retention Policy

## Purpose

Payment records must be retained long enough to support regulatory reporting,
customer dispute handling, reconciliation, and internal audit.

## Retention Rules

- Payment initiation records are retained for seven years.
- Settlement files are retained for seven years.
- Operational logs are retained for thirteen months unless linked to an
  investigation.
- Masked support transcripts are retained for two years.

## Handling Requirements

Payment data must be encrypted at rest and in transit. Access is limited to
authorized teams with a documented business purpose.
