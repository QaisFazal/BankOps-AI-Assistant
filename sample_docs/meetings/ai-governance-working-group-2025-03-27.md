# AI Governance Working Group Notes

## Attendees

Risk governance, legal, information security, data platform, compliance, and
digital banking.

## Agenda

The working group reviewed AI assistant access controls, prompt logging,
retrieval filtering, and human approval requirements.

## Decisions

- Every assistant response must include citations when enterprise documents are
  used.
- Restricted documents require explicit role checks before retrieval.
- Assistant traces must be retained for audit review.

## Open Questions

- Define retention periods for low-risk assistant conversations.
- Confirm whether model evaluation results should be reviewed monthly or
  quarterly.
