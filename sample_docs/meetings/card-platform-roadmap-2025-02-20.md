# Card Platform Roadmap Meeting Notes

## Attendees

Cards product, card platform engineering, fraud risk, enterprise architecture,
and operations.

## Topics

The group discussed authorization latency, commercial card controls, token vault
modernization, and fraud model rollout governance.

## Decisions

- Prioritize token vault resilience before new merchant analytics features.
- Require latency review for all new fraud features in the authorization path.
- Pilot commercial card controls with three corporate customers.

## Action Items

- Product to finalize pilot success metrics.
- Architecture to publish updated card processing diagrams.
- Fraud risk to document degraded mode decision criteria.
